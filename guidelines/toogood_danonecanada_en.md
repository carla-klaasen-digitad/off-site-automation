## Sugar Claims

- "80% less sugar" is acceptable in metadata only
- In all other content, frame as a comparison of lower-sugar yogurts — do not state "80% less sugar" as a standalone claim
- Do not use "low sugar" as a standalone claim — give exact content: "contains only X g of sugar per portion"
- Reference values: flavoured varieties contain 2 g of sugar per portion; plain contains 1 g per portion

## Vocabulary

- low sugar (standalone) → not permitted; use "contains only X g of sugar per portion"
- Follow all restrictions in general_danonecanada.md
