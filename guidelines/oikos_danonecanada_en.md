## Brand Rules

- Do not use "natural" — including for organic products; if organic, always prefer "organic" over "natural"
- Do not mention "a good source of potassium"
- Do not use "essential" when referring to products
- Do not target the keyword "full fat greek yogurt"
- Oikos' "30% less sugar" does not qualify for a "low sugar" claim — do not use "low sugar"
- Use "With high protein" (not "high-protein" — no hyphen)
- Do not say "healthy" → prefer "nutritious"

## Vocabulary

- natural → not permitted; use "organic" when applicable
- essential (re: products) → not permitted
- low sugar → not permitted even with the 30% less sugar claim
- high-protein → always "high protein" (no hyphen)
- Follow all restrictions in general_danonecanada.md
