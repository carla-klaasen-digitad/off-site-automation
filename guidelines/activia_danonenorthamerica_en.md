- **Quality Check:** NW Score Before / After, AI detection (gptradar.com), Plagiarism <10% (duplichecker.com)
- **Text highlighting convention:** White = original | Green = updates

### Product Page Content Structure
All Activia product pages follow this structure:

1. **Category tag** (above H1) — e.g., `PROBIOTIC CUPS`, `PROBIOTIC DRINKS`
2. **H1** — product name (e.g., "Peach Probiotic Low Fat Yogurt")
3. **Intro paragraph** — 2–3 sentences; flavor + probiotics + low fat; conversational, upbeat tone
4. **Pack size label** — `SINGLE` or `4` depending on SKU
5. **Feature bullets** (bold heading + 1-line description; use 3–5):
   - **On-The-Go** — "Skip the spoon. Just shake and sip." (drinks only)
   - **Low fat** — "Made with cultured Grade A reduced fat milk."
   - **Real [Fruit]** — describe real fruit ingredient (chopped or pureed)
   - **Creamy texture** — "A [dreamy/velvety] texture that keeps you [scooping/sipping]."
   - **Supports gut health** — "Billions of probiotics and active cultures in every [cup/bottle]."
6. **Nutrition Facts** (heading only — CMS populates)
7. **Ingredients** (heading only — CMS populates)
8. **FAQ section** — use "Got Questions About Our [Product]?" or "Frequently Asked Questions About Our [Product]:"
   - Always exactly **3 questions** per product page
   - Cover: probiotics/cultures strain, sugar content, storage/refrigeration, gluten-free status, calorie count
   - For flavor-specific pages, include a taste question ("What does [flavor] yogurt taste like?")
9. **Boilerplate footer** — "Good health starts with gut health" (three fixed H6 subsections: Gut-Brain Connection, Gut Microbiome, Diet for Gut Health)
   - ⚠️ **This block is being phased out.** It is currently crossed out on briefs. The SEO recommendation is to remove it and replace with the 3 product-specific FAQs above. New briefs should not include this block.

### Probiotic Strain — Always Reference Accurately
Full name: **Bifidobacterium lactis DN 173-010/CNCM I-2494**
Short form: **B. lactis DN 173-010/CNCM I-2494**
Use at least once per piece in FAQ or body copy.

### Sugar Language Rules
| ✅ Approved | 🚫 Never Use |
|---|---|
| `0g added sugar` | `0g sugar` |
| `Zero Added Sugar` | `zero sugar` |
| `0g of added sugars` | `sugar-free` (as a product claim in body copy) |

Note: `sugar free` is acceptable **as a target keyword** (what users search) but must not appear as a product claim in body copy — always translate to "0g added sugar" in copy.

### Drink vs. Cup Content Distinctions
| Drinks | Cups |
|---|---|
| "yogurt drink", "drinkable yogurt" | "yogurt cup", "spoonful" |
| "shake and sip", "on-the-go" | "grab-and-go", "breakfast or snack" |
| Feature: On-The-Go bullet | Feature: Sweetness/Real Fruit bullet |
