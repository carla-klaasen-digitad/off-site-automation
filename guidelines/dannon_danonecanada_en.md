## Brand Notes — Danone Yogurt (incl. Danino)

- Ingredients are "naturally sourced" — but never "natural"
- "Lactoseless" → always use "Lactose free" instead
- Avoid "irresistibly" — "Irresistible" is a competitor brand name

## Danino-Specific Rules

- Say "drinkable yogurt" for Danino, not "yogurt drink"
- Danonino is not cooked and does not go into recipes — do not suggest recipe use
- Children's products: no marketing addressed directly to children
  - Example refused by legal: "your kids will want to collect them all!"

## Vocabulary

- irresistibly → not permitted
- Lactoseless → use "Lactose free"
- natural (re: ingredients) → use "naturally sourced"
- Follow all restrictions in general_danonecanada.md
