## **1. Brand Overview**

**Brand:** REMIX Yogurt (United States)
**Parent Company:** Danone North America (Certified B Corporation™)
**Industry Sector:** Dairy / Yogurt with Mix-Ins
**Market Area:** United States

**Brand Description:**
REMIX is a Danone North America product line extension and campaign entity spanning three parent yogurt brands. It is not a standalone yogurt brand but a shared format — dual-compartment yogurt with a separate mix-in toppings compartment — applied under distinct nutritional platforms across Light + Fit®, Oikos®, and Too Good & Co.®

**The Three REMIX Sub-Lines:**

| Sub-Line | Base Brand | Nutritional Hook | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| Light + Fit® REMIX | Light + Fit | Up to 120 calories, fat free greek | 4.5 oz dual-compartment |
| Oikos® REMIX | Oikos | 11g protein, nonfat greek | 4.5 oz dual-compartment |
| Too Good & Co.® REMIX | Too Good & Co. | 60% less sugar, cultured ultra-filtered milk | 4.5 oz dual-compartment |

**Core Products & Flavors:**

*Light + Fit REMIX (Fat Free Greek Yogurt with Mix-Ins):*
* Strawberry Cheesecake (graham cookies, caramel pearls, dark chocolate)
* Brownie Sundae (brownie crumble, honey praline almond, butter toffee)
* Caramel Apple Snickerdoodle Pie (snickerdoodle pieces, white fudge chunks, cinnamon sprinkles)
* Caramel Apple Pie

*Oikos REMIX (Nonfat Greek Yogurt with Mix-Ins):*
* Cookies and Cream (cocoa cookie crumble, white fudge chunks)
* S'mores
* Salted Caramel
* Berry Dark Chocolate Crisp

*Too Good & Co. REMIX (Cultured Ultra-Filtered Milk with Mix-Ins):*
* Chocolate Covered Strawberry (chia granola, roasted almonds, chocolate pieces)
* Dark Chocolate Cherry Tart
* Peach Cinnamon Crisp

**Mission (Reflected in Content):**
The fun of mixing meets the smarts of better-for-you yogurt — indulgent flavors and textures you stir in yourself.

## **3. Target Audiences**

### **Primary Target — Indulgence-Seeking Health-Conscious Snackers**

* Age: 18–40
* Want a treat-feeling snack without the full caloric or sugar cost
* Drawn to dessert-flavored formats (cheesecake, s'mores, brownie) framed as "better for you"
* Sub-line self-selection: calorie-counters → L+F REMIX; protein-seekers → Oikos REMIX; sugar-avoiders → Too Good REMIX

* Oikos REMIX primary audience: active adults tracking macros, looking for protein-forward snacks that don't taste clinical
* "Snack strong" positioning — yogurt as fuel, not just food
* Attracted by 11g protein + dessert flavor combination

### **Tertiary Target — Parents & Family Snack Buyers**

* Ages 28–45
* Looking for fun, single-serve snack options that feel special
* Familiar with branded candy/cookie tie-ins (similar draw as YoCrunch)
* Portioned format appeals to household snack management

## **7. Editorial Tone & Style**

### **Tone to Use**

* Playful and energetic
* Snack-culture native — short punchy sentences, food-excitement language
* Casual and social-media adjacent — copy that could work as a caption
* Fun without being juvenile — skews 18–35
* Flavor-forward and sensory — lead with the taste and texture experience
* Confident about the better-for-you angle — state it without over-explaining

### **Tone to Avoid**

* Clinical or nutritional-report style
* Health-lecturing or virtue-signaling
* Exaggerated superlatives ("the best", "#1")
* Guilt-based messaging
* Long-form editorializing — this brand is punchy, not literary

---

## **8. Vocabulary Guidelines**

### **Preferred Terms**

* Mix-ins / mix in (core format descriptor)
* Toppings
* Stir / dig in (action language)
* Craveable
* Bold (Oikos sub-line)
* Snack strong (Oikos sub-line)
* Low calorie / up to 120 calories (L+F sub-line — factual only)
* 11g protein (Oikos — factual only)
* 60% less sugar (Too Good sub-line — vs. average yogurt with mix-ins)
* No artificial sweeteners (Too Good REMIX only)
* Live and active cultures
* Good source of calcium / protein (when nutritionally accurate)
* Fat free (L+F REMIX — factual only)

### **Words to Avoid or Exclude**

Never Use:
* Healthy (as a product claim)
* The best / #1
* Natural (use specific ingredient descriptions)
* Guilt-free
* Probiotic (use "live and active cultures")
* Sugar-free (use "60% less sugar" or "no added sugar" language per approved claim)
* Clean label
* Wholesome
* Nutritious (this brand leads with fun, not nutrition)

⚠️ L+F REMIX contains sucralose and acesulfame potassium — do not claim "no artificial sweeteners" for L+F sub-line. This claim applies only to Too Good & Co. REMIX.

## **9. Compliance & Mandatory Inclusions**

All content must:

* Use registered trademark symbols on first use: Light + Fit®, Oikos®, Too Good & Co.®
* Apply "60% less sugar" claim only to Too Good & Co. REMIX (vs. average yogurt with mix-ins benchmark — 155 cal / 4g fat)
* Apply "up to 120 calories" claim only to L+F REMIX
* Apply "11g protein" claim only to Oikos REMIX
* Apply "no artificial sweeteners" only to Too Good & Co. REMIX — NOT to L+F REMIX (contains sucralose, acesulfame potassium)
* Not claim gluten-free — L+F products contain wheat
* Not claim nut-free — Brownie Sundae contains tree nuts (almonds); cross-contamination risk exists
* Note milk allergen across all products
* Apply live and active cultures claim accurately across all sub-lines

**Standard FDA nutritional claim compliance applies throughout.**

## **11. Content Structure Requirements**

### **Product Pages**

* SEO-optimized H1 (sub-line + flavor name, e.g., "Oikos REMIX Cookies and Cream Greek Yogurt")
* Intro — 2–3 sentences; lead with the mix-in experience and flavor, then nutritional hook
* Feature bullets (3–5):
  * **[Mix-in description]** — name the toppings specifically
  * **[Nutritional hook]** — e.g., "11g Protein" / "Up to 120 Calories" / "60% Less Sugar"
  * **Fat Free** (L+F only) or **Nonfat** (Oikos)
  * **Live and Active Cultures**
  * **No Artificial Sweeteners** (Too Good REMIX only)
* FAQ section — exactly **3 questions** per product page
  * Cover: allergens, sweetener type (especially for L+F vs. Too Good), mix-in ingredients, serving and storage

### **Category / Landing Pages**

* Lead with the mix-in format concept — stir it yourself, your snack your way
* Organize by sub-line with nutritional hook for each
* Include all active SKUs per sub-line
* Include "Where to Buy" CTA

### **Recipe / Inspiration Content**

* Lead with the snack occasion or bowl build
* Highlight how REMIX mix-ins work as a base or component
* Keep it fun and visual — yogurt bowl builds, snack boards, after-school ideas
* Tag by sub-line where relevant (protein bowl → Oikos REMIX; low-cal bowl → L+F REMIX)
