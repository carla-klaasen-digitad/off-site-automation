Language: Canadian English

---

## Brand Voice

- SToK is a bold, no-fuss cold brew brand — tone should be direct and grounded, not aspirational or lifestyle-heavy
- Do not editorialize about coffee culture in a way that feels overly artisanal or boutique

## Product Naming

- Brand name: SToK (capital S, capital T, lowercase o, capital K)
- "cold brew coffee" — the primary product descriptor
- "ready-to-drink cold brew" — acceptable
- "black cold brew" and "decaf cold brew" — acceptable product descriptors
- "multi-serve cold brew" — acceptable
- Do not say "iced coffee" when referring to cold brew — they are different products
- Do not say "drip coffee" negatively in comparison

## Claims

- The 10-hour brewing process is a real product fact and can be mentioned as context
- Do not make caffeine-level claims (e.g., "high caffeine", "more caffeine than espresso") without verified data
- Do not say "the smoothest cold brew" — this is a subjective superlative
- Do not make health claims about coffee or caffeine
- "bold, smooth flavor" is acceptable brand language

## Vocabulary — Never Use for SToK

- iced coffee (as a synonym for cold brew)
- healthiest coffee
- most caffeinated
- artisanal (over-claiming on craft)
