## Product Naming

- "coffee creamer" is acceptable in English
- "Plant-based" cannot be used for International Delight products
- In the Iced Coffees there is no "real milk" — do not claim it

## Sugar-Free Claims — Approved Wording Only

Use only these exact phrases (no variations):
- sugar-free
- free of sugar
- no sugar
- 0 sugar
- zero sugar
- without sugar
- contains no sugar
- sugarless
- 0 g sugar
- zero g sugar
- 0 gram sugar
- zero gram sugar

## Vocabulary
