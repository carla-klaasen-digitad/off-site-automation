### Product Page Content Structure

> 🚫 **No FAQ sections on product pages.** Oikos product pages do not include FAQ sections. FAQs appear only in recipe/blog content (see below). This is confirmed by review of all on-site product brief docs.

1. **Size/SKU tag** (before or after H1) — e.g., `7 FL OZ BOTTLE`, `5.3 OZ Cup`, `QUART`
2. **H1** — product name in ALLCAPS for branded lines (e.g., "OIKOS TRIPLE ZERO® HIGH PROTEIN VANILLA GREEK YOGURT")
3. **Short intro** — 1–2 sentences; lead with protein amount; confident, energetic tone
4. **Feature bullet list** (ALL CAPS headers):
   - PROTEIN FOR MUSCLE MAINTENANCE* AND MUSCLE RECOVERY
   - PREBIOTIC FIBER TO SUPPORT DIGESTIVE HEALTH***
   - VITAMINS TO HELP CONVERT FOOD TO ENERGY
5. **NUTRITIONAL INFO** (heading only — CMS populates)
6. **INGREDIENTS** (heading only — CMS populates)
7. **Type 2 diabetes boilerplate** (yogurt products only):
   > "Eating yogurt regularly, at least 2 cups (3 servings) per week, may reduce the risk of type 2 diabetes according to limited scientific evidence."
8. **DESCRIPTION** — 2–3 paragraphs; expand on key benefits, flavor, convenience

### Recipe/Blog Content Structure
Use "Digitad Brief" as the tab header (not "Tab 1"). Recipe briefs differ from product pages:
- Include **Word Count** field with average competitor count benchmark
- Up to **6 internal links**
- Content: Intro → Ingredients (with macros table) → Directions (numbered steps) → Tips → FAQ (3–4 questions) → Client Information (social caption)
- Always use **Triple Zero Plain Nonfat Greek Yogurt** as the protein source
- Macros format: `Calories: X | Protein: Xg | Carbs: Xg | Fat: Xg`

### Meta Title Formula
Lead with protein amount: `[Xg] [Keyword/Product] | Oikos® [Line Name]`
Examples: `15g High Protein Blueberry Greek Nonfat Yogurt | Oikos® Triple Zero`
Must be 55–65 characters.

### Keyword Density Rule
Primary keyword: **5 times per 500 words**. Secondary keyword: **at least 2 times**.

### Internal Linking Patterns
- Product pages: 1–3 links within the same product line (e.g., flavor variants → category page)
- Recipe pages: up to 6 links — specific product used + related recipes + category pages
- All URLs begin with `https://www.oikos.com/`
