# Writing Guidlines: 
Lead with clarity, lighten with connection. 
## Guidelines
1. Approved claims give our copy its power because at the end of the day, parents want facts. They also want the assurance that Danimals offers the taste and texture their kids will enjoy. So, ensure all copy has a point that is either quantifiable, relatable or both.
2. Aim for impactful, inspiring lines without excessive length. Play with sentence structure. Be unexpected with vocabulary. Use clever wordplay as long as it isn’t wild, zany or childish and loses credibility with parents
3. We love a good exclamation point, but we’re not here to scream at people. Use exclamation points in headlines that benefit from an extra burst of energy or copy that you might actually shout (ex: SURPRISE!). Otherwise, use periods.
4. Use active voice, celebratory metaphors and real-time storytelling to draw readers in and encourage action. Avoid passive voice and language that feels too “cutesy.” Keep it clear, simple and relevant
5.  Danimals is a brand for kids, but we don’t talk like one. Our type of action is more about celebrating childhood moments versus regressing back to them ourselves or exclusively addressing kids
6. Provides a playful visual counterpart for Danimals copy 
7. Adds emotion and movement 
8. Makes copy feel more relatable and distinctively Danimals
9. NO First-person, narration or quoted dialogue Thought bubbles Over-the-top monkey language or catchphrases attributed directly to Bongo
## Examples of do's and dont's 
### To Include 
* Kids grow up so fast. Danimals keeps up with a good source of calcium, vitamin D and fiber in every delicious serving
* Like the first day of summer vacation, but sippable. Sweet strawberry flavor meets creamy banana flavor in a classic combo kids never grow out of
* PLAY-FULL. MESS-LESS
* SMART SNACKING? GROW FOR IT! ...and... GO, BONGO, GO 
* Superheroes need calcium and vitamin D to build strong bones too
* IT’S GROW TIME! 
### Exclude 
* What’s the best part of being a kid? Well…growing up, of course! Dreamy, creamy Danimals gives them what they need to do it.
    * Issue: Too vague. Lacks facts or context beyond “dreamy, creamy,” which leaves readers hanging
* Our strawberry banana flavor is so yummy! 
    * Issue: Lacks action, descriptiveness and originality.
* NO HIGH-FRUCTOSE CORN SYRUP! ...or... JOY IN EVERY SIP!!!     
    * Issue: Exclamation that feels “tacked on” or superfluous or use of multiple exclamations on one line.
* Because every superhero needs a smart sidekick. (Cape optional.) 
   *  Issue: Feels passive, lacks substance and reads somewhat cliché with the cape reference
* I’M GROWING WITH EVERY SIP! ...or... HEY, KIDDO! READY TO GROW? 
   * Issue: Feels too precious and kiddie. We build kids’ confidence with the nutrition they need to grow— not by acting like kids ourselves
