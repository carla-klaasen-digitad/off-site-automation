## **Business Overview**

Danone North America operates within the **food and beverage manufacturing sector**, with a focus on **health-forward consumer goods**. The company manages a diverse portfolio spanning **dairy, plant-based products, coffee, and specialized nutrition**, bringing together well-known brands under a unified corporate vision.

As part of a global organization, Danone North America plays a key role in advancing sustainable food systems while delivering products that align with evolving consumer expectations around health, transparency, and environmental responsibility.
